PHF Luxury Velvet Quilt Set

Indulge in the perfect balance of softness and sophistication with the PHF Luxury Velvet Quilt Set. Designed for those who seek both comfort and elegance, this king-sized quilt features premium velvet fabric on the top, offering a silky, cloud-like touch against your skin. The reverse side is lined with brushed microfiber, ensuring a cozy and breathable layer for every temperature. Whether it's a chilly night or a bright morning, this quilt adapts seamlessly to your needs.

Its intricate cross-stitch design brings a sense of refinement and artistry to your bedroom, making it a standout element in your decor. The sleek black solid color enhances the aesthetic, casting a captivating light and shadow effect that adds depth to your space. This set includes a bedspread measuring 108" x 92" and two matching pillow shams (20" x 36"), delivering a complete, stylish bedroom solution.

Ideal for year-round use, the PHF Luxury Velvet Quilt Set combines lightweight versatility with durability. The quilt is built with channel stitching and wrapped edges, ensuring long-lasting quality and a smooth finish. Plus, it's machine-washable and easy to care for, preserving its rich texture and appearance for years to come. Elevate your sleep with a touch of luxury and modern design.
